Special Rules date

The date the patient met the Special Rules could be from the date of diagnosis to when you complete their SR1 form.

Providing this means your patient gets paid from the correct date.

What date is the patient thought to have met the Special Rules?
If you do not know the exact date, you can use the first day of the month. For example, 1 10 1960.
