What happens next

Tell the patient you have sent the SR1 form.

Encourage the patient to make a benefit claim if they have not already.

This form is not a benefit claim and you have not made a benefit claim for your patient. The Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) will not contact your patient about making a benefit claim.

GOV.UK has advice for patients on making a benefit claim if they are nearing the end of life (opens in new tab) .

We will contact the patient when we have made a decision on their claim.

If we need more information about the patient, we will contact you.
