Get help with sending an SR1 form

General queries

The Personal Independence Payment (PIP) team manage enquiries for SR1 forms sent online, by email or by post. They can help with things like:

  • checking the progress of an SR1 form
  • providing a copy of an SR1 form
  • checking the progress of a benefit claim

    Telephone: 0800 121 4433

    Relay UK (if you cannot hear or speak on the phone): 18001 then 0808 157 0003

    Video relay service for British Sign Language (BSL) users - how to use the video relay service on mobile or tablet

    All services are available Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm

    Find out about call charges


Help sending forms by email

Email form.e-sr1@dwp.gov.uk to request an electronic version of the SR1 form and fee form.

You must email from a secure email address.


Help using the online service

The Send an SR1 medical evidence form team can help with things like:

  • accessing the online service
  • completing an SR1 form online
  • investigating technical issues

Email: online.sr1@dwp.gov.uk

We aim to respond within 3 working days.
