Send an SR1 form

Use this service to send an SR1 form to support a patient applying for a benefit with the Special Rules (opens in new tab).

The SR1 form has replaced the DS1500 form.

Use this service to tell the Department of Work and Pensions (DWP) that you have a patient who:

  • has a progressive disease
  • you would not be surprised if they were to live for less than 12 months

What you will need

You will need the patient's:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • address
  • diagnosis and other relevant conditions
  • date of diagnosis
  • date that they have met the Special Rules criteria
  • clinical features
  • treatment plan

We can process the patient's benefit claim faster if you include their National Insurance number.

You do not need to give a specific prognosis.

Use language that you would normally use when communicating with other clinicians.
